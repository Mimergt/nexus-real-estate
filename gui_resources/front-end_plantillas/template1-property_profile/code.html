Property Overview

Nestled in the heart of historic Antigua, Casa de los Sueños is a masterclass in blending colonial heritage with contemporary luxury. This expansive estate has been meticulously restored, offering an unparalleled living experience characterized by high-end finishes, soaring ceilings, and seamless indoor-outdoor integration.

The property centers around a stunning internal courtyard featuring a heated lap pool, lush indigenous landscaping, and multiple shaded alfresco dining areas. The chef's kitchen is equipped with top-of-the-line appliances, custom cabinetry, and a massive marble island, perfect for entertaining large gatherings.
